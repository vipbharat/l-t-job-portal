Platform Rules

Learn how to maximize your daily profits!

1
Recharge & Upgrade
To start earning, go to the Deposit page and add funds. Then, visit the Jobs section and Unlock a VIP Level (e.g., G2, G3) to activate your daily tasks.
2
Complete Daily Tasks
Log in every day and go to the Work Station. Click on the Submit Task button to instantly receive your daily VIP income in your wallet. Missed tasks cannot be recovered.
3
Withdrawal Rules
Minimum withdrawal is ₹200. Please note the withdrawal dates based on your VIP level:
• G1-G3: 1st to 10th of the month.
• G4-G6: 11th to 20th of the month.
• G7-G8: 21st to 28th of the month.
4
Build Your Team
Share your link from the Team page. When your friends join and upgrade their VIP levels, you earn a lifetime direct commission added straight to your balance!

⚠️ Strict Warning: Do not create multiple fake accounts on the same device. Our system tracks IP addresses, and cheating will result in a permanent ban and loss of funds.
